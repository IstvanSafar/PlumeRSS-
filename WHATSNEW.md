# What's New in Plume

**v1.1.0 - 2026-05-06**

---

- **New Feature**: Added swipe gestures for easier navigation. Swipe right on a feed to open its articles, and swipe left on the article list to go back.
- **Improved**: Unread article counts in the feed list now update instantly in real-time when articles are read, without needing a full sync.
- **Fixed**: Internal database optimizations to improve stability and ensure correct KSP compilation.
